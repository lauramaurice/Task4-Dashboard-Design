# Task4-Dashboard-Design
Interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing sales performance, KPIs, and business insights using the Sample Superstore dataset.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to design an interactive business dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales data and present meaningful insights for business stakeholders. The dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), explore sales trends, and make informed business decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## Dataset

* **Dataset Name:** Sample Superstore Dataset
* **Source:** Kaggle
* **File Format:** CSV
* **Records:** Approximately 9,994 rows
* **Columns:** 21

The dataset contains retail sales information, including customer details, product categories, sales, profit, quantity, discount, region, and order dates.

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive KPI Cards
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Category-wise Sales Analysis
* Region-wise Sales Distribution
* Segment-wise Profit Analysis
* Sub-Category Performance Analysis
* Interactive Slicers (Region, Category, Segment, and Order Date)
* Professional and Consistent Dashboard Theme

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance and help stakeholders monitor important business metrics.

---

## Visualizations Used

* KPI Cards
* Bar Chart
* Pie Chart
* Line Chart
* Column Chart
* Treemap
* Slicers (Filters)

## Dashboard Preview

## Business Insights

* Technology is the highest-performing product category in terms of sales.
* The West region contributes the highest sales among all regions.
* The Consumer segment generates the largest share of total sales.
* Sales trends vary across different time periods, highlighting seasonal business patterns.
* Interactive filters enable users to perform detailed analysis based on specific business requirements.
